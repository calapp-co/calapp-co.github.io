Getting Started

  1. Open Google Calendar
  2. Change to agenda view (top right, press SHIFT + A)
  3. Navigate to the days in your agenda that you would like to identify available times slots from
  4. Click    in the top right of Chrome

Hints & Tips

When you click CalApp by ShareAvailability.com, your availability is determined by which days are visible - so simply click any date in the future then click CalApp to generate your availability for that period.

Your availability is also calculated by which of your (or your colleagues') calendars you currently have turned on in in the bottom right of Google Calendar - turn on and off calendars to take them into or out of consideration.

If you more than one Google Calendar Accounts see below for how to easily share availability across them all.



How to share your availability across multiple of your own Google Calendar Accounts (e.g. your personal Google Calendar and your work Google Calendar)

Share one of your calendars with your other calendar (e.g. share your work calendar with your personal calendar) by following https://support.google.com/calendar/answer/37082



How to share your availability from your Apple/iCloud Calendar

Share your Apple/iCloud calendar with a free Google Calender by following https://help.practicebetter.io/hc/en-us/articles/115000669508-Linking-your-Apple-iCloud-Calendar-to-Google-Calendar



How to share your availability from your Office 365 Web Calendar

Scroll down and follow the instructions under "How to Import an Outlook Calendar into Google" to share your Office 365 calendar with a free Google Calender https://www.groovypost.com/howto/how-to-sync-outlook-calendar-with-google-calendar/



How to share your availability from your Office Desktop Application Calendar

Try one of the free/paid tools at https://www.makeuseof.com/tag/how-to-sync-microsoft-outlook-with-google-calendar/ (please let us know at outlook@shareavailability.com which method works best for you)
